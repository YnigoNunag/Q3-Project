Fire Prevention

  Smoke Detectors The installation of these devices can inform people in the household that there is a possibility of a fire
  Candles Extinguished When Leaving Room Candles may burn the wax below it in a way wherein it would fall after. If a candle tips over, flammable objects around it may set on fire leading to a bigger fire.
  Matches and Lighters Children are often very curious. This may lead to a match or lighter being accidentally used, thus leading to a fire. Make sure to keep them out of reach of children at all times to prevent a fire from starting.
